Essential notes

Before you go

Still to book
  • London hotel (5 nights, Aug 20–25) — near Soho/South Bank
  • Matera hotel (2 nights, Aug 25–27) — stay in Sasso Barisano or Caveoso for cave views
  • Taormina hotel (2 nights, Aug 30–Sep 1) — Mazzarò beach level or town; cable car connects
  • Matera → Rome train (Aug 27, ~10–11 AM) — book at trenitalia.com
  • Bari (BRI) car rental — pick up Aug 25 at airport, return in Taormina or Catania
Book in advance
  • Colosseum + Forum: coopculture.it — sells out days ahead in August
  • Vatican Museums: museivaticani.va — early access tour worth it
  • Borghese Gallery: galleriaborghese.it — mandatory timed entry, no walk-ins
  • Çemberlitaş Hamamı Istanbul: cemberlitashamami.com — ~€30 · 2 hours
  • Topkapı Palace: muze.gov.tr — Harem is a separate ticket
  • Turkish e-visa: evisa.gov.tr — ~$20, instant — buy before travel
Critical logistics
  • Aug 25: LGW → BRI departs 7:05 AM — leave London hotel by 4:30 AM · take National Rail or taxi to Gatwick
  • Sep 4: PMO → IST 9:25 AM — leave Alcamo Airbnb no later than 6:30 AM (PMO is 30 min from Alcamo)
  • Taormina ZTL: do NOT drive into old town — park at Lumbi, take cable car up (€3)
  • Sicily car: pick up at CTA airport Aug 30, return at PMO Sep 4 morning
  • Aug 27 Rome train: Matera → Rome journey ~4–4.5 hours — book specific timed seat
Practical notes
  • Currency: GBP in London · Euro in Italy · Turkish Lira in Istanbul
  • UK plug: Type G · Italy/Turkey: Type C/F · bring universal adapter
  • August Sicily heat: 30°C+ — start early, siesta 1–3 PM
  • Dress code: cover shoulders + knees for all churches and mosques
  • Matera: cobblestone sassi alleys — good shoes essential
  • Istanbul: staying with James — confirm exact address + key handover before travel
